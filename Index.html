Document Application FAQs

How to apply for Birth Certificate?

You need Aadhaar Card, Hospital Discharge Slip, and your Parents' Marriage Certificate. Visit your local municipal office or apply online at the official state portal.

Where can I get Income Certificate?

Income Certificate can be applied through CSC centers or government portals. Required documents include Aadhaar and income proof.

Can I apply for Domicile Certificate online?

Yes, most states offer online portals. You will need Aadhaar, residence proof, and a passport-size photo.

Is Jan Seva Kendra authorized for government services?

Yes, CSCs like Imran Jan Seva Kendra are part of the Digital India initiative and authorized to provide various citizen services.
